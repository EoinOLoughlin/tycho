User Persona: Major Corbin

Tactical UAV Mission Commander

Responsible for achieving mission-critical ISR and logistics objectives while minimizing risk to soldiers in contested, GPS-denied environments.

Goals & Motivations

Achieve >70% mission success; ensure personnel safety; gain tactical edge.

Pain Points

High drone attrition from GPS jamming; extreme operator cognitive load; unreliable systems under pressure.

Tycho AI provides a force multiplier. By automating navigation in contested airspace, it guarantees mission continuity, reduces operator burnout, and allows teams to focus on analyzing data, not just flying the drone. It transforms a high-risk gamble into a reliable tactical asset.

Go-to-Market Strategy & Roadmap

A phased, dual-use strategy de-risks market entry and maximizes long-term growth. The plan is supported by a multi-generational product roadmap focused on building a durable competitive moat with edge AI.

Phased Market Entry

Years 1-2
Phase 1: Prove & Penetrate

Focus on initial revenue and validation. Target the commercial Industrial Inspection market for early sales and case studies, while securing non-dilutive DoD R&D funding (SBIRs) to validate military requirements.

Years 3-4
Phase 2: Scale & Expand

Expand into adjacent commercial verticals (e.g., Construction, Public Safety). The key defense objective is achieving a DIU Blue UAS "On-Ramp" listing to unlock scalable procurement across the DoD.

Year 5+
Phase 3: Lead & Dominate

Address highly regulated markets like Urban Logistics. Transition to a key technology provider for major defense Programs of Record, cementing Tycho AI as the industry standard for autonomy in contested environments.

Product Roadmap

Gen 1: The Inspector

Robust VIO/SLAM engine for high-fidelity 3D mapping and pilot-assist navigation.

Gen 2: The Sentinel

Integrates event-based cameras for high-speed flight and on-device AI for object detection.

Gen 3: The Cognizant

Full Spatial AI engine for true environmental understanding and dynamic mission re-planning.
